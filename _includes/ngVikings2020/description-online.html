ngGirls Online is more than just a workshop. It’s an opportunity to connect, learn and grow.
In addition to following our tutorial, you’ll get:
  • Personal help from your mentor, with whom you’ll live-share your code
  • Code at your own pace, with an additional day to work on it and display your app
  • Lightning talks about Web apps and Angular
  • Quizzes, challenges and prizes!
  • Learn professional tips & tricks, as well as anything you’d like to know about being a Web developer
  • Connect with your group and continue working on additional features with the help of your mentor
  • Build and display your work - as part of your programmer’s portfolio, or just for fun!

The workshop is free of charge. Just fill out the 👉🏽application form.👈
